## Risk Management Framework

### Max Allocation Rules

- **Foreign total:** ≤ 50% of net worth (keep Thai base)
- **Any single country:** ≤ 30% (VN diversification)
- **Any single stock:** ≤ 15% (including DR)
- **Emergency fund:** Always 6 months Thai expenses in cash

### Stop-Loss Rules

**Don't panic sell unless:**
- Thesis broken (structural, not short-term)
- Fundamentals deteriorate (earnings decline 2+ years)
- Better opportunity requires capital reallocation

**Normal volatility:** Hold through ±30% moves

### Rebalancing

**Annual review:**
- Target allocation vs actual
- Rebalance if drift > 10 percentage points
- Consider tax implications of selling (capital gain)

## Emergency Playbook

### Market Crash (>30% drawdown)

- [ ] Don't panic sell
- [ ] Review thesis each holding
- [ ] Rebalance FROM bonds/cash TO equity if possible
- [ ] Buy opportunity if long-term story intact

### Currency Crisis (THB major move)

- [ ] Review FX-exposed positions
- [ ] Consider hedging (forwards) if >10M exposed
- [ ] Don't overreact to 5% moves

### Broker Failure

- [ ] SIPC ($500K US), SET protection (Thai)

- [ ] Transfer holdings if broker shutting down
